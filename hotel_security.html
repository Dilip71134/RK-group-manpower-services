Hotel Security Responsibilities.

At moshes fine food pvt ltd the safety and security of our guests, staff, and property are our top priorities. As a crucial member of our security team, your role is vital in maintaining a safe and welcoming environment. Below are the key guidelines and responsibilities for our security guards:

  • Patrol and Surveillance: Conduct regular patrols of the hotel premises, including guest floors, public areas, and parking lots. Monitor security cameras and ensure all areas are secure.
  • Guest Assistance: Provide a visible security presence while assisting guests with any inquiries or concerns they may have. Maintain professionalism and courtesy at all times.
  • Guest Assistance: Provide a visible security presence while assisting guests with any inquiries or concerns they may have. Maintain professionalism and courtesy at all times.
  • Access Control: Monitor and manage access to the hotel, including verifying guest and staff identification and ensuring that only authorized individuals enter restricted areas.
  • Documentation: Maintain accurate records of security incidents, daily activities, and any other relevant information. Submit reports as required.

    • Thank you for your dedication and commitment to maintaining a safe and secure environment at moshes fine food pvt ltd. Your role is essential in providing a positive experience for our guests and ensuring the safety of everyone on the property.

Stores Security Responsibilities.

At Central Sotres the safety and security of our guests, staff, and property are our top priorities. As a crucial member of our security team, your role is vital in maintaining a safe and welcoming environment. Below are the key guidelines and responsibilities for our security guards:

  • Surveillance and Patrols: Regularly patrol the store premises, including sales floors, stockrooms, and parking areas. Monitor security cameras and maintain a visible presence to deter theft and vandalism.
  • Customer Service: Assist customers and staff with inquiries, provide directions, and address any concerns while maintaining a friendly and professional demeanor.
  • Loss Prevention: Observe and report suspicious behavior or potential theft. Work with store management and law enforcement as needed to handle theft-related incidents.
  • Incident Management: Respond promptly to any emergencies or disturbances, including medical emergencies, altercations, or alarms. Follow store protocols for incident management and reporting.
  • Access Control: Monitor access points to the store, including employee areas and stockrooms. Ensure that only authorized personnel are allowed access to restricted areas.

    • Thank you for your dedication and commitment to ensuring a safe and secure environment at central stores. Your role is vital in providing a positive shopping experience for our customers and protecting our store’s assets.
